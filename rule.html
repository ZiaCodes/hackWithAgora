Rules of HackWithAgora

Welcome to the rules section of the hackathon. Kindly ensure that you go through them. In case of any queries, please send your query to sz2386@cse.jgec.ac.in

This is an individual Challange

Any project should be built during the hackathon itself.

participants can use libraries, frameworks, or open-source code in their projects.

Your project submission should adhere to the themes provided during the hackathon.

participants must submit their projects by 11:59 AM, 15th january 2022 over Github. Any projects being submitted after the deadline will not be accepted.

Projects that violate the Code of Conduct. will be disqualified and can no longer be a part of the HackWithAgora..


The projects would be judged on the basis of the following criteria :

  • Innovation/Creativity
  • Technicality
  • Completeness
  • Feasibility
  • User Experience
