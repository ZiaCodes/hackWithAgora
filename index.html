Frequently Asked Questions

I am not a student of JGEC can I participate

Yes, you can definitely participate.

I am a beginner can I participate?

There is no such restriction but with some amount of development knowledge in any domain will be good to go

Can I participate as a team?

No, this is an individual event

I don't know anything about Agora SDKs can I participate?

Then you must participate, because the aim of our event is to get more people into development.

I am not from CSE/IT Barnch Can I participate?

Yes, there is no such restriction.

What perks or Prize will I get?

5000 INR Cash for the 1st Prize as well as some special Goodies from Agora.io and there is also some swag and goodies for the runner up students.
